DAWAT IS CURRENTLY HIRING FOR THE FOLLOWING POSITIONS:


General Manager

Summary:


As a part of Team DAWAT Event Management , Your role will be to enhance and manage DAWAT’s services. Keeping in mind our roles and you will be expected to be the driving force of the company’s values and ethics. Together we are expected to present a cohesive voice for our brand DAWAT. You will be representing our company through all forms of communication and focused messaging.

Team DAWAT is seeking a General Manager who will be able to take up various job duties and will be able to lead our staff members based on a hybrid model of Agile and Traditional Methodologies. All departments will come under the supervision of the GM position.

The GM is required to report directly to the CEO daily on goals and reports, including sales goals, account invoices and forecasts. The GM should be ready to work weekends and should be used to long hours managing and supervising events.


Qualification:

Min Req. BA from an accredited university , Masters preferred. Have a “whatever it takes” attitude. Must be extremely organized and meticulous Local Karachite Sales and marketing experience a Must. Min Req 2 year Hospitality/Event experience Min Req. 6 years of minimum work experience Min Req 3 years of managerial role Marketing, branding and communication experience Experience in managing a team of at least 20 people in a fast paced environment Possesses extraordinary customer service, clear communication skills Experience with Contract Negotiation Proficiency in excel work and powerpoint mandatory Social media marketing mandatory Google Adwords a plus FB marketplace a plus Graphic designing a plus Working in agile environments a plus

Description:


Lead, plan and bring our clients vision to life by implementing creative event designs on the floor.

Find constant innovative ways to market our brand and enhance genuine focused leads to increase sales.

Lead the sales process in the company and regularly hold training sessions to improve staff goals.

Lead the vendor management side of the business by maintaining relationships and helping find solutions for problems that can create a communication barrier. Follow SOP for the company and hold the employees responsible for following it as well.

Encourage a creative and innovative atmosphere for team members to suggest ways to improve on our processes. Daily have a report and balance check in accounts for a smoother process. Build relationships with new clients and regularly stay in touch with our previous clients.

Make sure all company taxes are up to date and all legal government policies are followed. Focus on hitting sales targets with a focus on building value as well. Build and maintain relationships with our competitors and related event businesses such as hotels and photographers.

Regularly attend events for on floor company presence. Hold job fairs and fairly share job applications to the CEO for approval. Create and maintain communications in a harmonious and professional manner with all levels of staff and clients.

Treat all staff members with equality and fairness. Track budgets with vendors, agencies, clients and report to the CEO. Collaborate with all the teams and integrate them together towards the company’s goals and vision. Develop an annual budget and marketing strategy. Book hotel blocks through show organizers, and/or work with our corporate travel coordinator to lock in hotel blocks for upcoming shows

Identify roles that need to be filled within your team and assist the Employee Services department with hiring. (for example, trade show booth designer, production team, etc. Take calls with various companies, and corporations interested in working with our company.Experience of working within a demanding fast-paced environment upholding a very high standard. Advise clients on the solution that best fits their needs within their budget. Huge focus on client satisfaction. Travel to offsite locations, hotels, etc. – will include nights, weekends, etc. *note: could conflict with holidays

PLEASE NOTE THAT OTHER RESPONSIBILITIES AND DUTIES MIGHT BE REQUIRED AS THIS ROLE DEVELOPS OVER TIME.

Marketing Coordinator

You will be in charge of DAWAT’s social, mobile, and email marketing campaign. Candidate must have previous experience in the journalism field.

Must have past experience using multiple social media sites. Should have an understanding of email and mobile marketing. Should understand SEO marketing. Candidate must show creativity and a willingness to constantly learn more. An understanding of Google analytics, Adwords and video editing is a plus.

Qualification:

Must have BA from a regionally accredited University/College. At least 2-3 years of experience in event management or journalism related field.

P.R Coordinator

Qualification:
